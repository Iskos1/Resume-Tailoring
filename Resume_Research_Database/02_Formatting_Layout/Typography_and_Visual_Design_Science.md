## üîç Physics of Visual Perception

### **1. Contrast and Luminance**

**The Weber-Fechner Law:**
The perceived intensity of a stimulus is logarithmic to the actual intensity.

**Formula:** ŒîI / I = k (where k is a constant)

**Application:**

**Text-to-Background Contrast Ratio:**
- **Minimum:** 4.5:1 (WCAG AA standard)
- **Optimal:** 7:1 or higher (WCAG AAA standard)
- Black text (#000000) on white background (#FFFFFF) = 21:1 (ideal)

**Practical Guidelines:**
```
‚úÖ Excellent: Black (#000000) on White (#FFFFFF)
‚úÖ Good: Dark Gray (#333333) on White (#FFFFFF)
‚úÖ Acceptable: Medium Gray (#555555) on White (#FFFFFF)
```

**Physics Formula:** Œ± = 2 arctan(h/2d)
