## ðŸ” How ATS Systems Parse Resumes

### **The Parsing Process**

1. **File Conversion**
   - Resume is converted to plain text
   - Formatting is stripped away
   - System attempts to identify structure

2. **Field Extraction**
   - Name, email, phone number
   - Work experience (dates, titles, companies)
   - Education (degrees, schools, dates)
   - Skills and certifications

3. **Keyword Matching**
   - Scans for job-specific keywords
   - Matches against job description
   - Assigns relevance scores

4. **Ranking Algorithm**
   - Calculates match percentage
   - Ranks candidates
   - Filters out low scorers

### **What ATS Can't Do Well**
- âŒ Parse images or graphics
- âŒ Read text in headers/footers
- âŒ Interpret tables correctly
- âŒ Process text boxes
- âŒ Read columns reliably (left/right confusion)
- âŒ Understand creative formatting
- âŒ Extract data from PDFs with embedded images
- âŒ Parse special characters or symbols

## ðŸ”‘ Keyword Optimization Strategy

### **Understanding Keyword Matching**

ATS systems look for:
1. **Hard Skills** - Technical abilities, tools, software
2. **Soft Skills** - Leadership, communication, teamwork
3. **Job Titles** - Current and desired positions
4. **Certifications** - Professional credentials
5. **Industry Terms** - Domain-specific jargon
6. **Action Verbs** - Performance indicators

### **Keyword Research Process**

**Step 1: Analyze the Job Description**
```
1. Copy the entire job description
2. Highlight ALL requirements and qualifications
3. Identify repeated terms (= high priority)
4. Note exact phrasing (e.g., "project management" vs "managing projects")
5. Look for acronyms (include both versions: "SEO / Search Engine Optimization")
```

**Step 2: Categorize Keywords**
- Must-Have (Required qualifications)
- Nice-to-Have (Preferred qualifications)
- Industry-Specific (Domain knowledge)
- Soft Skills (Interpersonal abilities)

**Step 3: Strategic Placement**
Place keywords in:
1. **Professional Summary** (top section)
2. **Skills Section** (dedicated area)
3. **Experience Bullets** (contextual usage)
4. **Project Descriptions**
5. **Certifications Section**

### **Keyword Density**

**Optimal Strategy:**
- âœ… Use target keyword 2-3 times (not more)
- âœ… Use variations and synonyms
- âœ… Context is crucial - natural integration
- âŒ Avoid keyword stuffing (ATS can detect and penalize)

**Example:**
Instead of repeating "project management" 10 times:
- "Project management"
- "Managed projects"
- "Project coordination"
- "Project delivery"
- "PMP certified"

### **Common ATS Errors and Fixes**

| **Error** | **Cause** | **Fix** |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
